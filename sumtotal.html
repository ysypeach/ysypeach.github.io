In 2015 SumTotal made a shift from a company focused on feature count to an organization that is user focused. While their product was ranked one of the top Human Capital Management (HCM) solutions, SumTotal was losing marketshare over UX. The client approached York & Chapel and Momentum Design Lab with the hope of making their HCM suite intuitive and engaging to use. As one of the 18 designers on this project, I am responsible for the visual experience across Course Launch Player, Transcript, and Continuing Education. The product has been shipped and launched in 2016.

Client: Sumtotal (a skillsoft company)
Duration: five months, 2015
Team: UI & UX Designers, Information Architects, 
User Researchers, sumtotal internal Teams
My Role: User interface design, client communication
